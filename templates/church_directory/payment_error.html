Frequently Asked Questions

Was my card charged?

No, if the payment failed, no charges were made to your card. You may see a temporary authorization that will disappear within 1-2 business days.

Can I try a different card?

Yes, you can try again with a different payment method. Click "Try Payment Again" or return to the checkout page.

How long should I wait before trying again?

You can try again immediately with corrected information or a different card. If the issue persists, wait 15-30 minutes and try again.

What if none of my cards work?

Contact our support team for alternative payment arrangements. We can help set up bank transfers or other payment methods.

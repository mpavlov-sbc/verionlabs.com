Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my user limit?

We'll notify you when you approach your limit and help you upgrade to a suitable plan. Your service won't be interrupted.

Is my church data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular security audits, and comply with privacy regulations to protect your data.

Do you offer discounts for annual plans?

Yes! Annual plans save up to 17% compared to monthly billing and include additional features like priority support.
